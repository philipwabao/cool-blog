There is investor interest, and I am grateful for it.

The work is early. The model is showing real, emerging capabilities.

The right partner that I am looking for has to understand this early research:
what it can do, what it might do, and how much is still uncertain. With safety in mind, I have much more
to do to mitigate harm. That kind of partner is rare and worth waiting for.

Compounding the value of this research will bring stability to Auxerta and future
partners.
